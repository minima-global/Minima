Welcome

Chatter is an open decentralised censorship-resistant messaging network

Your messages are sent to your direct Maxima contacts

They can then choose to forward (re-chatter) those messages to their contacts.. and so on..

You can click on a Username and make them a Super Chatter

This means you automatically re-chatter messages they send

When you re-chatter a reply - they can request all the messages up the stack

You can use HTML in your messages.. so <b>BOLD</b> etc..

You can also embed images and certain other media..

<youtube>video_id</youtube>
<spotify_track>spotify_id</spotify_track>
<spotify_artist>spotify_id</spotify_artist>
<spotify_album>spotify_id</spotify_album>
<spotify_playlist>spotify_id</spotify_playlist>

You can PREVIEW your messages before you send them to see how they look

Happy Chatting!
